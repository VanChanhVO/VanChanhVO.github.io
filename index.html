Project Idea

Spending my youth for gaming most of free time such as Red Alert, Mega man X, Outlast, Super Mario or even Counter Strike... As we can see. from the beginning until today, there are many different types of games. Creating and developing a game are what I want to achieve. To develop a game, front-end developer and back-end developer will contribute and taking different of important roles. Developing a first-person survival horror game is what my project will be. Trying to survial and finding information of the killer in the city are the main reason for the character. The name of this game will be "NITE".

Curiosity and discovery are a human nature, searching for things they fear and going against mind are the reasons that "NITE" will be interesting and attracted. Furthermore, there are a big community of horror games strongly growing nowadays. Researching by Christina Gough, In 2018, United States consumer spent on games, content and hardware more than $43 billion US dollars (2).There are also many big markets for horror games that giving easy access for gamers such as Epic, Steam... Overall, with these informations, "NITE" will consolidate to make many gamer interesting and attractive.

Trying to survial and finding information of the killer in the city are the story of "NITE". "NITE" will be mix of horror and miniatur of violent, so "NITE" will be labeled over 16 (16+). The purpose of this game is attack into the mentality of players, which is make the players reaching the scare. However, making the players feel to play again and again after losing a part of the game or after horrible times will be the part of project by researching. Getting attention of gamers, aim of "NITE" will divide into two different parts. First, the environment impact will detail aspects such as sounds, smokes, roads, trees, type of houses and characters. Mentality of human are the second part of the project aim, it plays a role in affecting the player's emotions. Making sounds in "NITE" will make increasing fear of players, even before players meet the enemy or something terrifying. Souns of "NITE" will connect to some typle events to make players feeling worry and panic. Making players predict and anxiety is indispensable when making games of horror "NITE". Cover the player's character with smoke, fog and surround the character with a lot of footsteps or a bat flying past will be created to scare the players. That will make heaps of things running inside the player's brain. "NITE" will give the gamer many different conclusions, based on actions and attitude of the gamer. Once they get it wrong, players won't be able to come back and they will feel extremely sorry if things go bad. We will create a relationship between the player and the main character. Because of that, players will drop soul into the character. When their main character is in danger, they greatly increase the fear to the top.

Using Unity to make the game "NITE" is what we are going to create. "NITE" will be the 2D horror game with first view player. We will drawing the characters or some objects with CrazyTalk Animator 2. We also use Bolt Visual Scripting for create gameplay mechanics and interactive systems without writing code, so it will be more easier for making game. Skills required for making "NITE" are adapting to C#, C++ or JavaScript. Making a survey in public about the horror game to get comments and improve to respond to the market.

When the project would be successfull, we will develop "NITE" on PlayStation, Xbox, Nitendo and Linux version. As I said before, we will take more surveys to solve the prolems to adapt with the market.
